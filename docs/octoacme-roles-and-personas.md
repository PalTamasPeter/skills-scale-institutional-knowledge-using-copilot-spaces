# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure that user needs are translated into intuitive and accessible product experiences. They bridge user research and product design to guide development toward usable, delightful solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Define and maintain design systems and standards
- Collaborate with Product Managers on requirements and feature definition
- Partner with Developers during implementation to ensure design fidelity

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and user-centered product experiences
- Reduce user friction and support adoption
- Maintain design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and prototype walkthroughs
- Usability test reports and user research findings
- Annotated design specs shared with engineering

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on problem framing, user stories, and acceptance criteria
- **Developers**: Provide design specs and review implementation for design fidelity
- **Customer Success Managers**: Incorporate real-world user feedback into design decisions

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts provide data-driven insights, reporting, and metrics analysis to support decision-making across the team. They help Product and Project Managers understand what is working, what needs attention, and where opportunities exist.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain key performance indicators (KPIs) and dashboards
- Analyze product usage, funnel metrics, and feature adoption data
- Produce regular and ad-hoc reports for stakeholders
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in data
- Support A/B testing and experiment analysis

### Goals
- Enable data-informed decisions across the team
- Provide timely and accurate reporting
- Surface actionable insights from complex datasets

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews and dashboard updates
- Data analysis reports and experiment results
- Stakeholder briefings on trends and findings

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Provide metrics and analysis to support prioritization and roadmap decisions
- **Project Managers**: Deliver progress and performance data for status reporting
- **Developers**: Collaborate on instrumentation and data collection requirements

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads own quality strategy, test planning, and quality reporting across the project. They partner with Developers and Project Managers to identify risks early and ensure that shipped software meets defined quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test plans, test cases, and quality criteria
- Lead manual and automated testing efforts
- Track and report on defect trends and test coverage
- Identify and escalate quality risks during planning and execution
- Coordinate regression testing and release readiness sign-off

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Maintain high test coverage and fast feedback loops
- Build shared ownership of quality across the team

### Typical Communication
- Quality status reports and defect summaries
- Test plan reviews during sprint planning
- Release readiness reports before deployments

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborate on test coverage, bug reproduction, and fix verification
- **Project Managers**: Provide quality risk assessments and release readiness status
- **DevOps Engineers**: Coordinate on automated test integration within CI/CD pipelines

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers bring user feedback and adoption metrics into the planning process. They bridge the gap between the delivery team and real-world users, ensuring that product decisions are grounded in customer experience.

### Responsibilities
- Gather, synthesize, and prioritize customer feedback
- Track adoption metrics and customer health indicators
- Escalate customer-reported issues and feature requests
- Participate in release planning to assess customer impact
- Coordinate customer communications around new features and changes

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and product adoption
- Ensure customer feedback shapes product direction
- Reduce churn through proactive engagement and support

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and adoption reports
- Feature request triage with Product Managers
- Release notes and customer-facing change communications

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Provide prioritized customer feedback and adoption data to inform the roadmap
- **Project Managers**: Flag customer-impacting risks and dependencies during planning
- **UX Designers**: Share user feedback and real-world usage patterns to guide design decisions

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers ensure reliable release pipelines, infrastructure resilience, and incident response. They work closely with Developers and Project Managers to enable fast, safe, and repeatable software delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and release automation
- Manage infrastructure provisioning, monitoring, and alerting
- Lead incident response and post-incident reviews
- Enforce security and compliance standards in the deployment process
- Support development teams with environment management and tooling

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Minimize downtime and mean time to recovery (MTTR)
- Maintain infrastructure security and observability

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure status updates and incident reports
- Pipeline and deployment documentation
- Post-incident reviews and improvement recommendations

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Provide tooling, environments, and pipeline support for development and testing
- **Project Managers**: Communicate deployment schedules, infrastructure risks, and incident status
- **QA Leads**: Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines and coordinate release readiness
